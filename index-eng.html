ABOUT US

The history of Woodsystems began in 2009, when Oleh Kriuchkov took up furniture production. After 5 years of improvement of technologies and organizational issues he was joined by Yurii Pakhomov. The union of the organizer and the artist became crucial. The partners dedicates all their time to the company development. Mutual respect transformed into endless friendship, trust and dedication.
Later, key leaders and employees, without which the company would not have been the same, joined the team. Yurii’s creativity and Oleh’s organizational work together with outstanding support from the team is the secret of success of Woodsystems.

PHILOSOPHY

We are driven by the desire to achieve perfection.
The main principles of Woodsystems are the following:
being responsible, thinking about the future and introducing the new.
Learn what we are inspired by and become a part of the history of Woodsystems, having created our joint esthetic project.

STYLE

The basis of our style is uniting the limitless library of samples, from profile materials to exquisite exotic handmade samples in the Woodsystems products. Our mission is about understanding the customer, the elements they are inspired with and the surroundings in which they want to find themselves. Then we begin collecting the expectations of the customers in an individual concept. After the meticulous work of a large team is over, the customer gets the same image from their imagination and is able to touch this idea and possess it in real life, being inspired by this world.

INNOVATIONS

The sense of Woodsystems is about creating the future from the best things existing in this world in order to develop the best things that do not exist yet instead of waiting for the future. This is what innovations are – innovations in feelings, thinking, technologies, esthetics and shapes. It is our determination to be our own competitors every day. We are inspired by the change of patterns and stereotypes. Every concept comes to our laboratory, where we test the designs and give them new senses and functions. It all begins with an idea on a sheet of paper, which gains shapes and engineering prototypes, arguing with the laws of physics and technical capabilities of the equipment, and finally gets its place among our Woodsystems products.
