ABOUT US

We creatively and competently approach the implementation of the kitchen in your home. Our studio creates unique projects with taste. The main task is to create exclusive solutions. We listen carefully and find an individual approach to each customer.
Our designers work out every project down to the last detail. We will take into account all your wishes and surprise you with quality, creativity and comfortable design.

The history of Woodsystems began back in 2009, when Oleg Kryuchkov started working in the furniture business. After 5 years of honing technologies and organizational processes, Yuri Pakhomov joined him. The union of the organizer and the artist was a turning point. The partners devoted all their time to the development of the company. Mutual respect grew into endless friendship, trust and loyalty.

The team was later joined by key executives and employees without whom the team would not be the same. Yuri's creativity and Oleg's organizational activities, with the great support of the team, are the secret of Woodsystems' success.
