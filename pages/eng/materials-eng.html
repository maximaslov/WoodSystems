MATERIALS

Our Woodsystems hub has three template libraries:

1. Profile library - includes chipboard, MDF, painted facades, veneer, and other materials commonly used in furniture production.

2. Non-core (custom) library of samples - its peculiarity is in the detail and handmade work of craftsmen who make details only for a specific project, doing it in a single copy, forming the uniqueness of the product for the customer. These can be combined facades, leather, brass mesh, exotic materials, electroplating, metal anodizing, hand-embossed brass and many other techniques that add sophistication and special features to the kitchen.

3. Cyber library - components of which make Woodsystems kitchens more mobile, elegant and turn furniture into a gadget. The library includes GMO and caffeine analyzers, wireless phone chargers, sockets, phytolamps, quartzing of the sorting area, an electronic control module for better kitchen management, and many other devices. This is a transformation from ordinary furniture to a furniture gadget.
